About Me

I am a Graduate Teaching and Research Assistant at the Department of Construction Technology and Management, Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST), Ghana, completing my MPhil in Building Technology.

My research sits at the intersection of digital construction technologies, sustainable built environments, and circular economy — with a particular focus on how BIM, blockchain, and data-driven tools can transform construction practice in developing country contexts.

Beyond research, I am a practicing member of the Ghana Institute of Construction (MGIOC) and serve as UPG Sustainability Leader on campus.
