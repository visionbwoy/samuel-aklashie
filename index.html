About Me

I am a Graduate Teaching and Research Assistant at the Department of Construction Technology and Management, Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST), Ghana, completing my MPhil in Building Technology (GPA 3.83/4.00). I hold a First Class Honours BSc from KNUST — ranked 1st in Ghana in my department.

My research sits at the intersection of digital construction technologies, sustainable built environments, and the circular economy — with focus on BIM, blockchain, digital twins, and AI integration in the built environment. I actively mentor undergraduate and postgraduate students and contribute to editorial and conference organisation work.

I am a UPG Sustainability Leader, an Incorporate Member of the Ghana Institute of Construction (MGIOC), and an EDGE Expert trainee. I have co-authored public commentary pieces published on MyJoyOnline alongside Prof. Emmanuel Adinyira.
